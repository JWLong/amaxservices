– RPZ Backflow Testing & Repair

We're here to help

We test backflow prevention devices, which are important because they keep sewage from mixing with the fresh water. If the pressure of the main water line is reduced, sewage can creep in, with nasty results. In most circumstances, testing of backflow prevention devices is mandated by the city on a yearly basis. Typically, this affects business owners and residential homeowners who have sprinkler systems or pools.

What's an RPZ?

So... what is an RPZ anyway?

An RPZ is a bit of plumbing meant to keep sewage from seeping back into the fresh water. Proper maintenance keeps us from having to worry about such a possibility eleven months out of the year but, once a year we need to check it.
